Overview

Purpose

This site plan will have 3 games to virtually play. I will work on the ones that should function, and the plan is to have Uno, Chess, and some sort of a Brain teaser. I will make the last one fully from scratch and have various steps for it.

Audience

This is for people who want to paly games on the internet and to test the games there and to have fun.

Dynamic elements

I wanted to challenge myself with this task and will try and make the games fully interactive. This site could change drastically depending on the difficulties, but I will try my best to have working AI opponents for the Chess and Uno game with randomized card sets in the Uno game.

Home page

Welcome to a world where imagination meets strategy! Our tabletop gaming website is designed to bring excitement, adventure, and endless fun to players of all levels. Whether you're a seasoned strategist or a newcomer looking for your next great game, we've got something for you. Dive into thrilling game nights filled with tactical decision-making. Our featured games span a variety of genres—from fun family games to intense strategy showdowns—ensuring there's always something new to explore. With simply crafted game pieces, easy to use UI, and dynamic gameplay, each session is a unique experience. But the fun does not stop at the table! Join our vibrant community of gamers, where you can share your best moments, discuss strategies, and discover new favorites. Participate in tournaments, read expert guides, and connect with fellow enthusiasts who share your passion. With easy-to-learn rules, creative challenges, and endless replay value, this website will become your go-to hub for tabletop excitement. So gather your friends, sharpen your skills, and prepare for a fun and excitement. Your next great game night starts here!
